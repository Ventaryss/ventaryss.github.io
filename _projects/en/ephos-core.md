            </h2>
            <p class="text-lg text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-6">
                <strong>EPHOS Core</strong> is a comprehensive ERP platform designed to manage all operations of a cybersecurity company: client management, projects, audits, finances, communications, and external integrations.
            </p>
            <p class="text-lg text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-6">

            </p>
            <p class="text-lg text-gray-700 dark:text-gray-300 mb-6">
                The platform integrates <strong>ZENITH</strong>, a conversational AI assistant that helps users with their daily tasks, and offers over <strong>29 integrations</strong> with external services (Stripe, Slack, Google Drive, etc.).
            </p>
        </div>
    </div>
